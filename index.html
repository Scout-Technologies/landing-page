The Process

How Scout Works

Five steps. Zero manual work.

1

You Share the Candidate

Provide the candidate's details — experience, role type, location, and what they're looking for. That's all you do.

2

Scout Finds the Roles

Our AI scans job boards and company career pages every day to surface active, relevant openings — scored and ranked by fit for the candidate.

3

Scout Finds the Decision-Makers

For each role, we identify who's actually responsible for that hire — department heads, VPs, CHROs, talent leads. The right person, for that specific role.

4

Scout Reaches Out

Personalized outreach explaining why the candidate is a perfect fit for the role based on their experience + the JD goes out automatically via email and LinkedIn.

5

You Manage the Replies

Interested hiring managers reply directly to your email or LinkedIn. All you do is respond, qualify, and close. Scout handles everything before that.

Every job and contact is scored for relevance — Scout only targets the best fit jobs and highest-probability contacts.

FAQ

Common Questions

How quickly can we get started?
Fast. Once you send us a candidate's information, we can start reaching out to hiring managers within 24 hours. There's no lengthy onboarding or technical setup on your end.
What job boards and data sources does Scout use?
We cover all major job boards — Indeed, LinkedIn, Glassdoor, and more — plus we scrape company career pages directly. Our job coverage is massive. If a role is posted anywhere on the internet, we're going to find it.
Do we have to use the automated outreach, or can we just get the data?
Not at all — you can use Scout purely as a data source. We can send you daily lists of matched jobs and the hiring managers at each company, complete with their emails and mobile numbers. Finding accurate contact information is something we're particularly good at, and many of our customers use this data to power their own outreach workflows.
Do we have to advertise a specific candidate, or can we target more broadly?
No, a specific candidate isn't required. We can take a more general approach where we identify and target companies hiring for a certain type of role, without advertising a particular person. That said, we strongly recommend the candidate-led approach when possible — see below for why it works so much better.
Why is candidate-led outreach so much more effective?
After a lot of experimentation, we've found that response rate is 10x higher when you lead with a specific candidate. Think about it from the hiring manager's perspective — if you reach out without one, you sound like every other recruiter they ignore: "we don't work with recruiters" or "we already have 10 vendors." But if you say "I have a candidate with X background who looks like a great fit for the role you just posted" — now you have their attention. You're solving a problem they actually have right now. That's the entire philosophy behind candidate-led outreach, and it's why it converts.
How do you make sure the outreach doesn't hurt my brand?
Outreach is personalized per role and per contact — it's not a mass blast. We identify the right person at each company and craft messages relevant to that specific opportunity. Precision is built into how Scout works, not an afterthought.
What does it cost?
Pricing is customized based on the volume of candidates you'd like to run through Scout. The best way to get a sense of fit and cost is to book a call — we'll walk you through the data live and discuss what makes sense for your firm.
